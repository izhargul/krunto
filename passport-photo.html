How it works

1
Upload any photo — a selfie, phone photo, or scan all work fine.

3
Pick the exact size you need and download — ready to print or upload to a form.

Why use this tool

Passport and visa photos are rejected more often for framing than anything else — face too small, off-center, or the wrong aspect ratio. This tool locks the crop to the exact ratio required for each document type, so what you download matches the official size. Everything runs on your device, so your photo is never uploaded anywhere.
