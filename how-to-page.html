How to Fix a Blurry Phone Camera

A quick, practical guide — swap this content for any topic you want your page to rank for.

1 Clean the lens

Wipe the camera lens gently with a microfiber cloth. Pocket lint and fingerprints are the most common cause of blurry photos.

2 Remove your phone case

Some thick or poorly-fitted cases partially cover the lens or cause reflections. Take it off and test a photo without it.

3 Tap to focus

Before taking a photo, tap directly on your subject on the screen. This forces the camera to refocus on that specific point.

4 Check for condensation

Moving between hot and cold environments can cause internal condensation. If your camera looks foggy, let the phone sit in a dry room for a few hours.

5 Update your camera app

Outdated camera software occasionally causes focus bugs. Check your app store for updates to your phone's camera app or operating system.
