How this is calculated: Monthly payment = P × r × (1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n − 1), where P is the loan principal, r is the monthly interest rate (APR ÷ 12), and n is the number of monthly payments.

Good to know: APR (Annual Percentage Rate) includes both the interest rate and certain lender fees, so it's usually a more accurate measure of the true cost of a loan than the interest rate alone. This calculator uses APR to estimate your monthly payment.
