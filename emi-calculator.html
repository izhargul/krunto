What is EMI? EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed amount you pay every month toward a loan, covering both principal and interest, until the loan is fully repaid.

How it's calculated: EMI = P × r × (1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n − 1), where P is the principal, r is the monthly interest rate, and n is the number of monthly installments.
