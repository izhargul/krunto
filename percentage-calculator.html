Percentage Calculator

Three calculators in one — percentage of a number, percentage ratio, and percentage change.

How it's calculated

"X% of Y" multiplies Y by X/100. "X is what percent of Y" divides X by Y and multiplies by 100. "Percentage change" takes (Y minus X) divided by X, multiplied by 100 — a positive result is an increase, negative is a decrease.
